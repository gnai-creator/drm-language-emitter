# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and directly;
- focus criticism on technical content and evidence;
- disclose conflicts of interest when relevant;
- avoid harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks;
- respect licensing and attribution requirements.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- abusive or discriminatory language;
- deliberate intimidation or harassment;
- publishing private information without permission;
- misrepresenting research results, benchmarks, or safety claims;
- submitting work that knowingly violates third-party licenses.

## Research Integrity

This is experimental research software. Claims about performance, safety, geometry, or model capabilities should be supported by reproducible evidence.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when this code of conduct is violated.
